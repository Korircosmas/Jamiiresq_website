About JamiiResQ

JamiiResQ Solutions is a tech-driven platform designed to reduce emergency response time in Kisumu County. Our mission is to empower local responders, integrate communication systems, and ensure that emergencies are handled swiftly and efficiently across communities.

The Problem

Communities in Kisumu face critical delays in medical, fire, and disaster response due to uncoordinated communication, inadequate responder networks, and limited access to real-time emergency services. These delays lead to preventable fatalities, worsening injuries, and increased trauma.

Our Solution

JamiiResQ leverages mobile technology, geolocation, and a network of trained community responders to reduce these delays. The platform integrates emergency reporting via smartphone, SMS, or USSD, and intelligently dispatches responders based on proximity and availability. We also partner with healthcare facilities and security providers to improve emergency outcomes.

Impact

Through pilot programs, we expect to reduce average response times by over 40%, improve survival rates, and increase public trust in local emergency services. Impact is measured using real-time analytics, feedback surveys, and field reports from users and responders.
